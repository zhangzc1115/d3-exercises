Overview

This page consists of working solutions for 3 simple exercises of varying degrees of difficulty done for an Information Visualization class using d3.

Exercise 1 is a simple bar chart.

Exercise 2 consists of a two-dimensional array dataset of 3 varaibles and two functions. Clicking on the text will scale the chart 1/3, 2/3, and full height, respectively. Clicking on any of the bars will sort the bars according to the nth variable, in order.

Exercise 3 consists of a geomap of the US.
